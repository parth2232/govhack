The COVID-19 pandemic had a significant impact on education in New South Wales (NSW). Schools and universities were forced to close or switch to online learning for extended periods of time, disrupting the education of millions of students. The impact was felt across the entire education system, from early childhood education to tertiary education.

Impact on Student Educational Outcomes:

The pandemic affected various aspects of education, including retention/enrolment rates and HSC completions. Many students faced challenges in adapting to online learning, which could have impacted their educational outcomes.

Lessons Learned:

The experience of the pandemic highlighted the importance of preparedness for future crises. To minimize the impact on education for future pandemics, it's crucial to develop robust online learning systems, provide adequate support for students, and enhance communication between educational institutions and students.
